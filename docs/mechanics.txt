# TechnoloJesus - Core Mechanics

## 1. Expanded Expense Categories
To enhance financial realism, *TechnoloJesus* will feature various expenses beyond salaries:
- **Food & Groceries** – Affects employee morale and productivity.
- **High-Speed Internet** – Impacts work efficiency.
- **Power Bills** – Cost increases with server usage.
- **Office Rent & Upgrades** – Balances cost vs. employee comfort.
- **Recreational Expenses** – Includes cannabis, psychedelics (creative boosts/side effects), gym memberships, team events.
- **Miscellaneous** – Includes coffee, snacks, software licenses, remote work stipends.

## 2. Market Competitor Mechanics
### Chinese Knockoff Apps
- If the player develops a successful app, a Chinese competitor may create a near-identical version at a lower cost.
- Knockoffs can steal users, forcing innovation, lawsuits, or aggressive marketing.
- The player can also reverse-engineer knockoffs to create their own clones.

### Pakistani Click Farms
- Players can hire click farms to generate fake engagement.
- **Pros:** Rapid growth, investor attraction.
- **Cons:** Risk of app store bans, negative press.
- AI-generated reviews may have hilarious broken English.

## 3. Background Dice-Roll System
A hidden **1d100 roll** determines key gameplay outcomes, modified by player decisions:
- **Funding Pitches:** Randomized investor responses.
- **Employee Productivity:** Daily morale fluctuations.
- **App Market Success:** Viral potential varies.
- **Competitor Actions:** Randomized feature copying, sabotage, or failures.

## 4. Employee Enthusiasm System
Employees have an **Enthusiasm Bar** (0-100), affecting efficiency:
- Enthusiasm drops over time.
- Low enthusiasm reduces skill effectiveness and increases quit risks.
- Players must balance workloads, perks, and passion projects.

## 5. [UPPER MANAGEMENT] Mode
A high-level strategy mode focusing on employee oversight. Features:
- **Employee Task Tracking:** Completed, uncompleted, current, and next tasks.
- **Needs Tracking:** Hunger, enthusiasm, "brodom" (boredom), stress.
- **Performance Tracking:** Effectiveness ratings, skill progression.

## 6. Hiring/Firing System
Inspired by *GTA V* crew hiring:
- Each job offers **3-4 candidates** with:
  - **Proficiency** – Skill level.
  - **Mood** – Current emotional state.
  - **Quirks** – Traits like "Perfectionist" or "Burns out easily."
- Recruitment strategies include:
  - Standard job listings
  - Poaching competitors
  - Intern hires (cheap labor)
  - **Emergency Freelance Hire** for short-term, high-cost replacements.

## 7. Employee Performance Reviews
Performance ratings are based on:
- **Task Completion Rate**
- **Code Quality & Efficiency**
- **Creativity & Innovation**
- **Collaboration & Communication**
- **Loyalty & Work Ethic**
- **Consequences:** High performers demand raises; low performers face probation or firing.

## 8. Live Social Media Updates
A dynamic system where the public reacts to in-game events.
- Posts (positive, neutral, negative) affect reputation.
- Employees may post about pay, working conditions, or company drama.
- Investor confidence fluctuates based on sentiment.

## 9. Daily Summary Report
At the end of each in-game day, the player receives:
- **Project Progress**
- **Employee Enthusiasm Changes**
- **New Expenses**
- **Social Media Sentiment**
- **Market Trends**
- **Major Events (Layoffs, hirings, breakthroughs)**
- Players can choose **highlight view** or **detailed report** mode.
