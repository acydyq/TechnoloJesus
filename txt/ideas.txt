# 🎮 TechnoloJesus - Spontaneous Ideas List (Fully Updated)

## ✅ Recent Changes:
- **Competitive Rivalries:** AI companies now compete with and sabotage each other, not just the player.
- **Employee Stats:** Employees now start with stats **between 51% and 83%**, making optimization a challenge.
- **Minimum Daily Revenue Task:** Each employee **automatically generates small revenue daily**.
- **Expanded Revenue Streams:** Players can earn money through **app development, selling user data, and shady dealings**.
- **All dollar amounts are rounded to the nearest hundredth place ($567.89).**
- **Non-salary expenses (e.g., rent, utilities) are calculated per property, not per person.**
- **Recurring expenses are automatically deducted at the end of each in-game day.**

---

## 1️⃣ **Core Startup Simulation Mechanics**
- The game is a **startup simulation/management** experience where the player runs a tech company.
- Inspired by *Silicon Valley*, featuring **app/game development, employee management, office upgrades, skill trees, industry rivalries, and blockchain integration**.
- Built with **Python (Pygame, Pygame GUI, PyTMX, SQLite)**.
- Uses **isometric pixel art** for visuals.

---

## 2️⃣ **Expanded Expense Categories**
- **Food & Groceries** – Employees need to eat, affecting morale and productivity.
- **High-Speed Internet** – Essential for efficiency, with tiered costs.
- **Power Bills** – Affects servers and office equipment.
- **Office Rent & Upgrades** – Now calculated **per property**, not per person.
- **Recreational Expenses:** 
  - Cannabis and psychedelics (creativity boost or side effects).
  - Gym memberships (health/morale boost).
  - Team outings and events (team synergy).
- **Miscellaneous** – Coffee, office snacks, software licenses, work-from-home stipends.
- **💰 Recurring Expenses:** Automatically deducted at the **end of each in-game day**.

---

## 3️⃣ **Competitive Rivalries (AI vs. AI)**
- Rival startups **compete and sabotage each other**, not just the player.
- AI-driven companies engage in:
  - **Market Takeovers** – Stealing users/clients.
  - **Corporate Espionage** – Trade secret theft.
  - **Negative PR Campaigns** – Fake bad press.
- The player can **observe rivalries unfold or manipulate them**.

---

## 4️⃣ **Chinese Knockoff Apps**
- **Mechanic:** If the player develops a successful app, a Chinese company may create a near-identical version at a lower cost.
- **Effects:** The knockoff could siphon users away, forcing the player to innovate, sue (expensive), or compete on marketing.
- **Strategic Element:** The player can also reverse-engineer knockoffs to make their own clones of trending apps.

---

## 5️⃣ **Pakistani Click Farms**
- **Mechanic:** Players can hire Pakistani click farms to artificially boost downloads, engagement, and app store rankings.
- **Risk vs. Reward:** Risk of app store bans, negative press, or losing credibility if exposed.

---

## 6️⃣ **Background Dice-Roll System**
- **Mechanic:** A **1d100 roll** determines certain outcomes, with modifiers based on skills, experience, and strategy.
- **Use Cases:** Funding pitches, employee productivity, app market success, competitor actions.

---

## 7️⃣ **Employee Enthusiasm System (Inspired by 'State of Decay 2')**
- **Employees never start at 100%** in any category.
- **Initial stats range between 51% and 83%.**
- **Enthusiasm Bar (0-100)** – Affects efficiency, **Flow State (boosts at high enthusiasm), Burnout (penalties at low enthusiasm).**
- **What Affects Enthusiasm?**
  - 🔼 **Increases:** Pay raises, office perks, recreation, passion projects.
  - 🔽 **Decreases:** Overworking, salary disputes, failed projects.
- **Encourages skill progression through training, mentorship, hands-on projects.**

---

## 8️⃣ **[UPPER MANAGEMENT] Mode**
- **High-level management mode** focusing on all employees.
- **Employee List Sections:**
  - **Last Completed Task**
  - **Last Uncompleted Task**
  - **Current Task**
  - **Next Task**
  - **Time Since Last Break**
  - **Time Since Last Day Off**
  - **Hunger**
  - **Brodom** (boredom – needs to hang out with friends doing recreational activities like smoking pot, taking shrooms, drinking Ayahuasca, tequila, etc.)
  - **Performance Rating**
  - **Skill Progression**
  - **Stress Level**
- **Sims-style mechanics** for dynamic employee behavior.

---

## 9️⃣ **Hiring/Firing System (Inspired by GTA V Crew Hiring)**
- **Crew Hiring Mechanic:**
  - Each job presents a **small pool of candidates (3-4)**.
  - Employees have **randomly assigned ratings** for:
    - **Proficiency** – Skill level in the job.
    - **Mood** – Current emotional state.
    - **Quirks** – Special traits (e.g., "Perfectionist," "Burns out easily").
  - The player picks one candidate, making hiring **a strategic choice**.

- **Recruitment Strategies:**
  - **Standard job listings**
  - **Poaching top talent** from competitors
  - **Hiring interns** for cheap labor
  - **Emergency Freelance Hire** – When a crucial employee quits, the player can bring in a freelancer for a **short-term, high-cost contract**.

---

## 🔟 **Employee Performance Reviews**
- **Performance Metrics:** 
  - Task completion rate
  - Code quality
  - Creativity
  - Teamwork
  - Loyalty
- **Affects:** Salaries, promotions, company reputation, potential layoffs.
- **Salary is influenced by skill level and performance metrics.**

---

## 1️⃣1️⃣ **Minimum Daily Revenue-Generating Task**
- Each employee will **automatically complete at least one revenue-generating task per day**.
- **Prevents "dead days"** where no money is made.
- **Revenue is small** but ensures **consistent engagement**.
- The task type depends on their role:
  - **Coders:** Bug fixes, minor updates, optimization.
  - **Marketers:** Small ad campaigns, social media promos.
  - **Sales Team:** Minor sales pitches, lead generation.

---

## 1️⃣2️⃣ **Multiple Revenue Streams**
- The game will feature **diverse ways to generate income**, including:
  - **App Development & Launch** – Traditional revenue from app sales, ads, subscriptions.
  - **Selling User Data** – Gray-area revenue model, risky but profitable.
  - **Shady Dealings** – Black-hat strategies such as:
    - **Fake engagement farming** (click farms, bot traffic)
    - **Crypto/NFT scams** (pump-and-dump schemes)
    - **Pay-to-win microtransactions** (if making mobile-style apps)

---

## 1️⃣3️⃣ **Live Social Media Updates (Inspired by Cities: Skylines 3)**
- Employees, consumers, and industry insiders **post live updates** about the company.
- **Dice-roll system** determines if posts are **positive, neutral, or negative**.
- **Impact:** Influences **company reputation, employee morale, and investor confidence**.

---

## 1️⃣4️⃣ **Daily Summary Report**
At the end of each in-game day, the player gets a **summary of the day's events** with an option for a **detailed report**.

### 🔹 **Summary Includes:**
- **Project Progress**
- **Employee Enthusiasm Changes**
- **New Expenses**
- **Social Media Sentiment**
- **Market Trends**
- **Major Events** (Layoffs, firings, hiring, major breakthroughs)
