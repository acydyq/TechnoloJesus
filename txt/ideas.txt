End of Day Confirmation System
At the end of each in game day the player will be prompted with the choice to 

Option 1 End the Day
- A few in game hours pass then the next day begins
- Any unfinished tasks carry over to the next day
- The player starts the new day fresh with updated stats finances and news

Option 2 Continue Managing
The player can take various actions before advancing time such as
- Hiring and Firing Employees Adjust workforce size and talent
- Seeking Investment Pitch to investors or negotiate funding
- Checking the Stock Market Buy sell shares analyze trends
- Viewing Financial Stats and Reports Review earnings debt and performance
- Adjusting Project Priorities Reassign tasks or modify strategy
- Making Strategic Business Decisions Evaluate risks expansion opportunities or new ventures

Time Does Not Advance Until the Player Chooses
- The next in game day will NOT begin until the player actively confirms the decision
- This ensures full control over daily operations investments and project planning
